A Little About Me

I was born and raised in Oklahoma, but when I enlisted in the army I got stationed in Alaska and have been there since 2018. I was with an airborne combat team and got to do some cool stuff, but when covid hit we were ordered to spend much of a year locked in very small rooms. To stay sane I created a schedule with all sorts of activities and eventually got really into programming, and it has stuck with me since! I ended my service in February and have been working as a ski instructor and glacier/wildlife conservation guide while waiting to take on a course like this. Although my background may make me seem to be a bit of a meat head, I spend quite a bit of my freetime PC gaming, obsessing over to many different fandoms, and nerding out over different scientific topics. Feel free to reach out if you want to get to know me better!
